About Me

Hello. My name is Sean Whittaker and i am studying to become a web developer in the Rutgers Full-Stack Bootcamp. For the past 3.5 years, I have been in kitchens itching my love for cooking. But, eventually I realized it's not something I can do forever with my life. After sitting down with my family we all thought it would be great for me to take a shot in the technology world just to see how I feel about it and I can say so far it's been a great transition. It gives me feelings of being back in the kitchen, but along with new struggles that are so much more rewarding. When they are worked out and the whole website comes together, its like that recipe that just finally hits on all flavor levels.
